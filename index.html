About Us

Precision in every movement, decision, and play.

Accelerate Performance Football was built on the belief that junior players deserve access to professional-level coaching, and senior athletes deserve better training options than what was available.

We focus on bridging the gap between aspiring young talent and elite athletic development. Every session is tailored to the individual, built around their position, their weaknesses, and where they want to go.

What We Offer

Four programs. One goal.

Every program is built around private coaching. We tailor each session to you, not a group template.

01 Ages 6+

Junior Prep

Getting younger players ready for senior-level football. Drilling fundamentals until they become second nature.

  • Ball control and first touch under pressure
  • Passing precision - short, long, weighted
  • Game awareness and football IQ
  • Confidence building through visible progress
Learn more
02 Experienced Players

Senior Refinement

For players who already understand the game and want to level up. Refine what makes you dangerous.

  • Technical and tactical assessment
  • Advanced ball control and passing accuracy
  • Tactical positioning and field reading
  • Match-ready conditioning and mental resilience
Learn more
03 Off-Season

Off-Season Conditioning

The break between seasons is where real gains happen. Gain an edge while others rest.

  • Aerobic endurance and explosive power
  • Agility and directional change
  • Technical skill maintenance
  • Arrive at preseason in peak form
Learn more
04 Recovery

Injury Return

Structured recovery that gets you back stronger. We work alongside your medical team to rebuild strength and confidence.

  • Strength rebuilding and match fitness
  • Weekly check-ins and program adjustments
  • Clear milestones in speed, strength, confidence
  • Progress is measured, not guessed
Learn more
