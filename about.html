About Us

Players first.
Coaches second.

Built on the belief that every player deserves access to professional-level coaching.

Our Story

Precision in every movement, decision, and play.

Accelerate Performance Football was built on the belief that junior players deserve access to professional-level coaching, and senior athletes deserve better training options than what was available.

We focus on bridging the gap between aspiring young talent and elite athletic development. Every session is tailored to the individual, built around their position, their weaknesses, and where they want to go.

How It Works

Three steps. One goal.

01

Assess Your Level

First session evaluates your current ability, decision-making, and physical conditioning. This gives us a clear picture of where you sit.

02

Build Your Plan

A personalised training plan targeting weaknesses and position-specific skills. Every drill has a purpose, every session moves you forward.

03

Track Progress

Ongoing 60-minute sessions with measurable progress every step of the way. We track improvement so you can see the results of your work.
